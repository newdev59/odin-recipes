Steak Recipe

Description

Wonderful sous-vide steak

Ingredients

  • Sirloin Steak
  • Olive Oil
  • Kosher Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Fill sous-vide container with water and heat to 120 degrees
  2. Place steak in zip lock bag with olive oil, salt, and pepper
  3. Sous-vide for 3 hours
  4. Heat cast iron skillet to 375 degrees
  5. Sear steak in butter for about 1 minute each side
